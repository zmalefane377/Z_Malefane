Introduction

This section focused on developing creative and technical skills in Computer-Aided Design (CAD). It emphasized the use of digital tools to translate ideas into accurate models. A key learning point was understanding the difference between vector and raster graphics. Vectors allow precise, scalable designs, while rasters are resolution-dependent and best suited for images. This knowledge supported effective and accurate digital design work.
