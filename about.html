About Me

Zandile Malefane is an emerging clean energy innovator and entrepreneur from Lesotho, currently pursuing a Civil Engineering qualification at Lerotholi Polytechnic. She volunteers and serves as an innovator at SeboPi UniPod, contributing to research and development projects focused on sustainable technologies, particularly waste-to-energy systems that address environmental challenges in Lesotho and other countries. In addition to her engineering and innovation pursuits, Zandile is committed to STEM education and youth empowerment, serving as a robotics coach and mentor to inspire young learners to embrace science and technology. Her professional interests lie at the intersection of sustainability, infrastructure development, and community advancement, with a vision to create practical, scalable solutions that improve quality of life and protect the environment locally and globally.
